Questions

Gutter Cleaning in Riddle — FAQs

How often should gutters be cleaned in Riddle?

Twice a year minimum under Riddle's tree cover — after fall leaf drop and again in spring. Homes under heavy fir canopy often need a mid-winter check too, since needle litter keeps coming down through the storm season.

What happens if I skip gutter cleaning in Riddle?

Overflowing gutters rot fascia boards, dump water against the foundation, and can back water up under the roof edge. A $200 cleaning skipped becomes fascia replacement, drainage work, or a roof-edge repair — the most avoidable expensive problem in the county.

Do you repair gutters too, or just clean them in Riddle?

Both. Sagging runs, failed hangers, leaking seams, and crushed downspouts are all repairable, and we'll flag fascia rot before it spreads. If a run is past saving, we'll say so and quote a replacement honestly.
