Our Story

Family-owned, community-minded.

Mistletoe Construction is a family-owned roofing and construction company founded by local expert Alex Smith. We prioritize community values and customer satisfaction, and we're proud to be an Owens Corning contractor — one of the only manufacturer-certified roofing companies based in Douglas County.

We're a licensed and trusted Oregon contractor (CCB #255729), committed to delivering high-quality work you can count on — from a single repair to a full replacement, and everything in between. We live and work in the same communities we serve. When we say we treat your roof like our own, we mean the roof down the street from ours.
